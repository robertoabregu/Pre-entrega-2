LAMBORGHINI

AVENTADOR SVJ

INTERIOR

The technological aspect of carbon fiber, present in the monocoque and taken up again on the doors, seats and center console, coexists with an aeronautical-inspired design in which leather and Alcantara® upholstery give the luxurious interior a more decided touch. But it is the refined details of the cross stitching and the internal SVJ plate that make the character of this new mechanical jewel unique.

ENGINE

The Aventador powertrain features a mid-mounted naturally aspirated 6.5-liter V-12 that develops 769 horsepower. The engine's 531 pound-feet of torque makes its way to all four wheels via a seven-speed automated-manual transmission.
